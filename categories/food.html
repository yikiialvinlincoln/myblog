Matooke

Matooke is a popular Ugandan dish made from steamed green bananas. It is often served with a sauce made from groundnut or peanut paste.

Ugali

Ugali is a staple food in Uganda, made from maize flour cooked with water to a thick porridge-like consistency. It is often served with a variety of sauces and stews.

Luwombo

Luwombo is a traditional Ugandan dish made by steaming meat, chicken, or fish with vegetables in banana leaves. It is cooked slowly to enhance the flavors.

Rolex

Rolex is a popular street food in Uganda, consisting of an omelette rolled in a chapati (flatbread). It is often filled with vegetables and sometimes meat.
