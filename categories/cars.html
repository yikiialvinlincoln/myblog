Lamborghini

This is one of the most expensive cars in the world. It has an engine size of 6.5 liters and can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds.

Ferrari

Ferrari is known for its performance and luxury. It typically features a V8 or V12 engine, delivering exceptional speed and power.

Bugatti

Bugatti cars are renowned for their speed and luxury. The Bugatti Veyron Super Sport, for example, has a top speed of over 400 km/h.

Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a legendary sports car known for its distinctive design and powerful performance. It typically features a rear-mounted flat-six engine and offers exhilarating driving dynamics.

Mercedes-Benz S-Class

The Mercedes-Benz S-Class is a luxury sedan known for its refined elegance and advanced technology. It offers a spacious and comfortable interior, along with a smooth and powerful driving experience.

BMW M5

The BMW M5 is a high-performance sedan that combines luxury and sportiness. It features a powerful engine, precise handling, and a comfortable interior, making it a top choice for enthusiasts.
